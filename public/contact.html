❓ Common Questions
Is Theo's Fight really free?

Yes, completely. We will never charge families to use this service.

Are the grants you find real?

Yes — we search for real UK grants from genuine charities and organisations. We always recommend verifying details directly on each organisation's website before applying, as information can change.

Do you store my child's information?

No. We do not store any personal information. Everything you enter is used only to search for grants and is never saved. Read our full privacy policy for details.

Can you help if the search doesn't find anything?

Yes — email us at hello@theosfight.co.uk and we'll do our best to help personally. Sometimes a human search is needed for more complex situations.

Can I apply to more than one grant at a time?

Absolutely — and we encourage it. Most grant organisations are aware that families apply to multiple sources and it is completely acceptable to do so.
