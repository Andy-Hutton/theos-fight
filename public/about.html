Built by a family, for families

Theo's Fight was created by Andy Hutton, whose son Theo inspired every part of this project. We know what it feels like to be in your position — because we've been there too.

💙 Our Story

When Theo was diagnosed, our family stepped into the unknown. Every appointment, every assessment, every piece of equipment — it all came with uncertainty, paperwork, phone calls, waiting lists, and costs that nobody fully prepares you for.

We threw ourselves into fundraising. We called our campaign Theo's Fight — because that's exactly what it felt like. A fight. Not against Theo's condition, but against the systems, the waiting times, and the financial barriers that stand between disabled children and the equipment they need.

We knew not every family has the network, the energy, or the time to fundraise — especially when you're already stretched to your limits just getting through each day. That's why we built this.

When starting out on our family's journey, every step was into the unknown. Appointments, diagnosis, equipment, time frames and costs are all causes of stress. Let us help take a little bit of that pressure away.

— Andy, Theo's dad & founder of Theo's Fight

So we built this. A free tool that does the searching for you — finding real UK grants for specialist equipment, and helping you apply with a personalised letter written around your child's unique needs and personality.

No assumptions. No jargon. No cost. Just a little bit of the pressure taken away.

🐒 Why the Monkey?

The monkey holding the T has been Theo's symbol from the very beginning. He featured on every piece of fundraising material, every social post, every event. He became part of our family story.

When we built Theo's Fight, it felt only right that he came with us. He's a reminder of where this all started — and a promise that the spirit of Theo's original campaign lives on in every family we help.

🔒 Our Promise to You

We will never charge families to use this service. Ever.

We will never store, sell, or share your personal information or your child's medical details.

We will always describe your child using your words — never assumptions or generalisations.

And we will always be honest about what this tool can and can't do. We help you find grants and apply for them. The rest of the fight is yours — but you don't have to start it alone.
